Our Process

Dream

Our dedicated team of professionals will insure that the vision of your oasis comes to life. We believe every outdoor space has the potential of becoming your dream retreat, a place to enjoy with family and friends and make lasting memories. We will help you create your unique space.

Design

At Clover Industries, we take immense pleasure in designing spaces. Whether you have a design in mind or need assistance, we'll work together to achieve your dream space.

Our Design Process...

  • Utilizes Land FX (one of the best landscape design programs available)
  • We fly a survey drone to measure the space with pinpoint accuracy
  • Gathered data is loaded into the Land FX program, using AutoCad and Revit software
  • Creates a detailed 3D design, bringing your project to life with stunning detail

The possibilities for your dream space are endless with our innovative design approach!

Build

Now the fun part begins! After discussing your goal and designing your space we will begin the installation.

Our Building Process...

  • Consistant updates on your project
  • Set deadlines
  • Commitment to your satisfaction
  • Ensuring every project meets your expectations

Initial Excavation

insures proper nutrient content in the soil, correct grades for proper runoff, and proper bedding before any plants are installed

Sprinkler Systems

are engineered for optimum efficiency, installed using top-quality components, and as-built designs given to clients afterward

Arrangement

of your plants from our designer will be the final process before installation to ensure it is aesthetically pleasing.

Crafting exceptional landscapes is our passion. We give your project the care and attention it deserves.
